Abstract


Abstract is an assessment I did for the first term of my graphic design diploma studies. I was to create a poster and a brochure for a hypothetical exhibition. The A and many of the letters used are made out of stock images of milk splashes with a colourful effect blended on top of them. The milk splashes were individually puppet warped to construct the A.

This eye with colourful paint flying into it was used for the brochure, the brochure is a fold-out spread I created it so that once you open the brochure up this image will be the first thing that's seen.

This was featured on the back of the brochure, all of the letters were individually made to retain the main point of the hypothetical exhibition.
